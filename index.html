Bitwise Operators and Their Uses

Bitwise operators are fundamental in programming for manipulating individual bits in binary representation. They include:

  • & - Bitwise AND
  • | - Bitwise OR
  • ^ - Bitwise XOR
  • ~ - Bitwise Complement
  • << - Left shift
  • >> - Right shift
  • <<< - Unsigned Left shift
  • >>> - Unsigned Right shift

Applications

  • Optimizing Code: Use bitwise operations for efficient manipulation of flags and settings.
  • Setting/Clearing Specific Bits: Easily modify individual bits for various purposes.
  • Working with Binary Data: Perform bitwise operations on binary data for tasks like encryption.
